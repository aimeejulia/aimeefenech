Board Membership


Stitching Reculture Foundation

Stichting Reculture Foundation is a Dutch public benefit organisation offering fiscal hosting in Europe, supporting projects that are fundraising in euros and engaging with EU funding contexts.

On the board, I focus on the work that keeps a fiscal host trustworthy and functional:
  • Supporting financial administration and day to day governance
  • Interviewing prospective hostees and leading onboarding
  • Strategic planning, improving the systems that help projects fundraise responsibly and operate transparently

I care about fiscal hosting being more than paperwork. Done well, it protects projects, reduces risk, and makes it easier for good work to be funded without compromising values.
