Board Membership

Stichting Reculture Foundation

I sit on the board of Stichting Reculture Foundation, a Dutch non profit and public benefit organisation that provides fiscal hosting in Europe. The foundation supports projects that fundraise in euros and those seeking EU grants, offering the administrative and governance structure that enables projects to receive and manage funds responsibly.

My current responsibilities include:

  • Financial administration support: strengthening how money is tracked, processed, and reported so hostees and funders can rely on clear records
  • Hostee interviews and onboarding: assessing fit, clarifying expectations early, and helping new hostees start well
  • Strategic planning: improving our policies and internal systems so the foundation can host projects with integrity as we grow

This role suits me because I enjoy the intersection of values, governance, and real world operations.

If you are looking for a fiscal host in Europe, you can contact us at contact@reculture.foundation.
